Recommendation III-VI (ATCM III - Brussels, 1964)

QUESTIONS CONCERNING MEETINGS OF SPECIALISTS

The Representatives, 

recognizing the importance of the problem raised during the examination of Item 7 (that is, the Item entitled "Questions concerning Meetings of Specialists"), 

recommend their Governments to examine this question attentively before the Fourth Consultative Meeting and to consider including it on the Agenda of that Meeting.
