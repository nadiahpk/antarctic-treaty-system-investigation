Recommendation IV-20 (ATCM IV - Santiago, 1966)

INTERIM GUIDE LINES FOR THE CONSERVATION OF FAUNA AND FLORA

The Representatives recommend to their Governments that, until such time as the Agreed Measures on the Conservation of Antarctic Fauna and Flora may become effective in accordance with Article IX of the Antarctic Treaty, the following Recommendations as far as feasible be considered as guidelines in the interim period: 

Recommendations IV-1 to IV-19 inclusive.
